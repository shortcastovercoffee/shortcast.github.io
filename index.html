About me

Hello, my name is Bala Devarajan, and I'm the host of the podcast Shortcast Over Coffee. My life has always been driven by an insatiable level of curiosity. From my earliest memories, I've been captivated by questions like, "Why are certain things the way they are?". This quest for understanding has not only shaped my personal journey but also inspired the creation of this podcast. Through Shortcast Over Coffee, I've had the extraordinary opportunity to engage with a diverse array of remarkable individuals from various backgrounds. These conversations have enriched my understanding and perspective, and I hope they do the same to you too. Please do like, share and subscribe the podcast on your favorite medium. Feel free to reach out to me at shortcastovercoffee@gmail.com. with feedback, guest suggestion or even a "hi". I am an engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area.
